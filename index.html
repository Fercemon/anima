FUTURE FOR FISH

WHAT ARE WE UP TO?

We want to raise standards for fish in aquaculture by

  • demand humane methods of stunning before slaughter.
  • regulate stocking densities to levels that provide better fish welfare.
  • regulate fish transport.
  • invest in research focused on main farming species actual needs, as very little is known about the living conditions for those fishes in terms of their natural behaviour and welfare.
